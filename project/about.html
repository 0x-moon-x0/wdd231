About Us

What We Make

Here, in the Mary Made Stuff store, we offer a wide variety of handmade crafts, such as toys, jewelry, accessories, and more! Our small team of creative minds each has a unique talent that they use to bring ideas to life.

We Offer...

  • Jewelry: Bracelets, earrings, rings, anklets, and more, all handcrafted with a variety of beads, metals, and stones.
  • Toys: Lovingly crafted crochet and sewn toys.
  • Baby Car Seat Covers: Soft, custom designs to keep little ones cozy.
  • Accessories: Headpieces, cosplay props, pins, hair accessories, and unique items to complete any look.
  • Seed Bead Crafts: Charms, intricate jewelry, and more made from seed beads.
  • Clothing Repairs and Tailoring: Thoughtful fixes and custom tailoring for a perfect fit.
  • And More: Each piece is one-of-a-kind, designed to add a bit of magic to your day.

Customization

At Mary Made Stuff, we believe every item should be as unique as the person receiving it. That's why we offer customization on each of our products!

Customers can select colors, materials like beads and stones for jewelry, and fabrics. If you have a vision or a photo for inspiration, bring it along, and we'll do our best to create a piece that fits your style perfectly.

Our Team

Our team's mission is to bring joy and creativity into every piece we make. At Mary Made Stuff, we're here to make your dreams come true, one handmade item at a time.

Nothing brings us more happiness than creating items that delight and inspire our customers!
